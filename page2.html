Video Games 101

More About Gaming

Video games continue to evolve with new technology, including virtual reality and cloud gaming. They offer immersive experiences and creative storytelling that rival movies and books.

Many games also foster social connections, whether through online multiplayer or esports tournaments. Gaming can be both fun and a way to develop skills like teamwork and strategy.

Interesting Facts About Gaming

  • The first video game is considered to be "Pong" in 1972.
  • Esports tournaments can offer prize pools of millions of dollars.
  • Some games are used in education and training for problem-solving skills.
