Modern video games include virtual reality, cloud gaming, and competitive esports.

Gaming can build skills like teamwork, problem-solving, and creativity while providing entertainment.

Interesting Facts About Gaming

  • The first video game is considered to be Pong (1972).
  • Esports tournaments can offer prize pools of millions of dollars.
  • Some games are used in education to teach problem-solving skills.
